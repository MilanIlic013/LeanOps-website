Services
What I do
Operational clean-ups & transitions
Blockchain and AI companies move fast and break things — including their own operations. I help teams regain structure and momentum during growth spurts, pivots, acquisitions, and reorgs.
Process simplification
Most fast-moving tech teams carry more process debt than they realise. I identify what's blocking execution, clarify ownership, and reduce the friction that quietly drains your team's energy.
Cost & efficiency reviews
Whether you're optimising a lean team or unwinding a bloated structure, I examine how money, time, and effort are being spent — and where simple changes unlock real gains.
Finance & operations coordination
In crypto and AI, Finance, Legal, and Ops rarely speak the same language. I bridge that gap — facilitating smarter decisions and ensuring nothing falls between the teams.
AI & automation integration New
I help companies cut through the AI noise: identifying where automation genuinely saves time, implementing workflows that actually stick, and avoiding the trap of buying tools nobody uses.
Fractional & advisory ops support
Not every team needs a full-time COO. Short-term, hands-on support providing teams with additional operational capacity or clarity — stepping back when you've found your footing.

Methodology
How I work
01
Understand before prescribing
I spend real time learning how your company actually operates — not how the org chart says it does. The real blockers are usually different from the stated ones.
02
Prioritise ruthlessly
We identify the two or three issues genuinely slowing you down and work on those first. No 90-day transformation frameworks. No slide decks that sit in a folder.
03
Solutions your team will actually use
Everything I design is built for real people under real pressure. Simple, clear, and practical — not impressive on paper but ignored in practice.
04
Hands-on when it counts
I'm not an advisor who disappears after the kickoff call. I work alongside your team during implementation — especially during the hard parts.
05
Clear scope, honest communication
You'll always know what I'm working on, what it'll take, and what to expect. No surprises, no scope creep, no wasted time.

FAQ
Common questions
What kind of companies do you work with?
Primarily blockchain, Web3, DeFi, and AI product companies — from early-stage startups to organizations with 100–500+ employees. I'm most useful when operational complexity is outpacing internal capacity.
Are you a fractional COO?
Sometimes that's the right label, sometimes not. I work in whatever capacity makes sense: short-term project-based engagements, ongoing advisory, or embedded part-time support during a specific transition.
Are you open to full-time roles?
Occasionally, yes — for the right company and challenge. If you're looking for someone fully embedded, fully committed, and not split across multiple clients, that conversation is worth having. Full-time means your team gets all of my attention, all of the time. Reach out and let's see if it's a fit.
Do you work remotely?
Yes — I work with distributed teams across time zones. Most of my clients operate globally by nature.
How quickly can you start?
Usually within 1–2 weeks of an initial conversation. For urgent situations, often faster.
What's your pricing model?
Engagements are scoped and priced per project or on a monthly retainer basis, depending on what makes sense. We discuss this in the initial call — there's no fixed price list because every situation is different.
